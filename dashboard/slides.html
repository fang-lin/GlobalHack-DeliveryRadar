why this matters

Two paths, one loop

👩‍💻
Today — humans write the code

The loop works naturally: every PR is checked against recorded intent, drift is surfaced, implicit decisions get captured. Intent and implementation keep converging — instead of drifting apart one green build at a time.

🤖
The exploration — autonomous agents

Long-running coding agents drift from intent over hours of work. Machine-checkable intent gives them a self-check loop: verify against the decisions before opening a PR, capture new decisions as they're made, escalate to a human only at decision points. This is what makes long-horizon autonomy governable.

🔍
Every step is tracked and auditable
verdicts carry evidence + constraint IDs · every human confirmation is recorded · intent history lives in version control — you can always answer who decided · what changed · why
