why this matters

From writing, to steering, to autonomy

👩‍💻
PAST
Humans write the code

Intent lives in people's heads and meeting rooms. Drift is slow enough that human review mostly keeps up — alignment by craftsmanship.

🕹️
TODAY
Humans steer agents in real time

Agents write the code; alignment holds because a human watches every step and corrects drift live, prompt by prompt — alignment by supervision. It scales to one person, one session.

🤖
THE EXPLORATION
Long-horizon autonomy

Recorded, machine-checkable intent replaces real-time steering: the agent self-checks before opening a PR, captures new decisions, escalates to a human only at decision points — alignment by infrastructure. The human steps out of the loop; alignment stays in.

🔍
Every step is tracked and auditable
verdicts carry evidence + constraint IDs · every human confirmation is recorded · intent history lives in version control — you can always answer who decided · what changed · why
